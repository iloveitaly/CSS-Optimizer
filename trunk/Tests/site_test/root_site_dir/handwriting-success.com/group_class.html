Amy Carroll, OTR/L is providing small group handwriting instruction after school on Wednesdays. This is an ideal time for students to work on their handwriting skill development with a small group of their peers.

Students will enjoy periods of individualized handwriting instruction followed by fun group activity breaks designed to support growth in their foundation skills.

The Handwriting Without Tears Program will be used unless otherwise discussed with parents/teachers of individual students.

Children will be provided with a handwriting book suited to their skill level and/or age.

A small amount of homework will be assigned to encourage integration of new skills.

Classes:

Wednesdays (except holidays)

Class A : 3:15 – 4:15 pm
Class B : 4:30 – 5:30 pm
  • Students will be grouped according to their age and/or style of handwriting (print or cursive).
  • Because instruction is individualized, Students can join the class at any point during the year.
  • The number of classes children attend depends on their individual needs.

Cost: 40.00 per class
