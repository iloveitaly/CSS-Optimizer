Summer is the perfect time for children to improve their handwriting for the next school year! Handwriting Success's summer camp provides a structured and fun program in an upbeat "camp" atmosphere.

In a typical 2 hour class, students alternate handwriting instruction with engaging activities that support growth in the foundation skills needed for handwriting

Foundation skills include: pencil grasp, fine motor, visual-perceptual motor, sensory awareness, eye-hand and gross-motor coordination, strength, attention and self esteem.

The child friendly Handwriting Without Tears program is an excellent curriculum for children of all ages.

Small class size allows for individual instruction within a group setting.

Class Offering:

Pre-Writing Class: for pre-k to K

This class focuses primarly on developing foundation skills for handwriting. Children will also be introduced to letters through a variety of pre-pencil to paper techniques. Letter formation with a pencil will be introduced according to each students readiness.

Printing Class: Grades K-2

This class is for students who are ready to learn printing or to imprive printing habits. Some of these habits include pencil grip, letter formation, letter to line placement, sizing and spacing of letters. Printing speed will be addressed when appropiate for older children.

Cursive Class: Grades 3-5

In this class students will be provided with instruction to enchance cursive writing skills including: learning proper pencil grip, letter formation, placement of letters on the line, and connection strokes and when appropriate, handwriting speed and fluency.

Advanced Cursive: Grades 3-5

In this class students who have mastered the basics of cursive writing will work on speed, fluency and getting organized thought onto paper.

Middle School Group

In this 1 hour 15 minute class students will be provided with instruction to enhance print and/or cursive writing skills. They will also learn pencil grip, letter formation, placement of letters on the line, and connection strokes. When appropriate, speed, fluency and higher level writing skills (i.e. note taking, writing content organization) will be addressed.
