Ness' Profile

One of Puerto Rico’s foremost leading persons of the 1990’s.  Ness Díaz Valentín represents an inspiration to photographers and human beings all over the world.  He has managed to make his mark with the international audience as well.  Born in the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico on September 6, 1966, Ness is the only child of a civil engineer and a stay-home mother.  He received elementary, junior high, and high school education in Puerto Rico.  In addition, he completed two years of college in Puerto Rico.  Díaz Valentín completed a Bachelor of Arts degree at West Chester University.

Since he majored in Philosophy, Ness received training in Ethics, Logic, and Aesthetics.  This has enabled him to better understand the way we think, to better test our reasoning, and to best appreciate beauty.  The studies required for completing the Philosophy program, such as courses relating to the philosophy of religion, philosophy of history, and philosophy of science, has helped him to learn how to analyze the way people think and communicate.

By receiving a minor in Peace and Conflict Studies, Ness was able to learn how to examine social conflict, conflict resolution, and group communication on a national, as well as international level.  It also contributed to his understanding of peace with justice, various functions of conflict, and processes through which conflict can be managed.  In addition to the concentrations in Philosophy and Peace and Conflict Studies, he received a Liberal Arts education, completing courses relating to humanities, visual arts, languages, social science, economics, communication, health, math, science, law, photography, business, computer, and music.

Ness’s PHOTOGRAPHY; what he refers to as WEDDING/EVENT PHOTONATURALISM; “a lifestyle vision alive with ideas, creations and versatility...it entitles couples to personal attention, throughout all the different stages of their day; the house/hotel facility coverage, the ceremony coverage, the park/facility coverage, the reception coverage—the entire day.”  True to essence, Ness Díaz Valentín stands for a lasting value.  Within its heraldic crest are the elements integral to his name: honor and trust.  “It’s a brand mark, a label, and above all, a reflection of one’s Journey, your Journey.”  He sees photography as a way of being.  More than just Traditional Film or Digital Film, Medium Format, 35mm or Digital cameras, Black and White or Color, it is about being better, being you, being the Best you can be, not only in a career but in all aspects of life; all those Journeys we travel in this place we call Universe.”

His aesthetic style is both journalistic/documentary as well traditional in spirit.  It takes inspiration from Classic Photographers styling tradition such as: Ansel Adams, Denis Reggie, David Beckstead,  Dan Light/Jeff Spence/Andy Bianco -Ultimate Images Photography and Video, Monte Zucker, Phil Kramer, Jery D, Mike Colón, John Kellar, Karina María Díaz, Ryan Joseph, Kelly O’Keefe-Kelly O’Keefe Photography, Paul Csihas; as well as all of those photographers throughout the world...all bringing sophistication and refinement to his own Personal Style.  The Ness Díaz Valentín BRAND defines an approach to Weddings/Events that’s about the Photographer’s ability and talent to blend different styles, techniques, equipment, personalities, environments to create images that not only are beautiful but images that are layered in complementary ways to create a distinctive style.

Ness believes in tradition.  He loves Weddings/Events, vibrant people, people of all walks of life—“I’m a people oriented person.  I like to create and conserve friendships of all kind.  My photography is the key that unlocks versatility in my Wedding and Events.  My approach can provide couples with everything they need to transition from their Engagement Imagecreations to their Wedding Imagecreations and beyond—we begin establishing some kind of professional friendship.  Exaggerated posing is never used; thus, offering ease and freedom that every couple can appreciate”.

Díaz Valentín has developed a wide variety of techniques and approaches to execute his classically inspired and sophisticated IMAGECREATION.  His personal IMAGECREATIONS are about style, approach, versatility and personality: “it’s about combining styles, approaches, techniques, talents, information, in complementary ways to create a distinctive and a personal style.
