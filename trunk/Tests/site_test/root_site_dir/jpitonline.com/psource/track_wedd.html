IMPORTANT AND HELPFUL TIMELINE SUGGESTIONS TO KEEP YOU ON TRACK

THE DAY OF YOUR SPECIAL MOMENT

  • Make sure the best man and the maid of honor sign the marriage certificate
  • Be sure to eat and drink properly
  • Allow enough time to get dressed
  • Prepare for you hairdresser and makeup appointments
  • Enjoy your wonderful wedding

THE DAY BEFORE YOUR WEDDING

  • Rehearse ceremony with attendants
  • Update seating chart
  • Reconfirm transportation to/from ceremony and reception
  • Attend rehearsal dinner
  • Get a manicure or a massage
  • Stay with your family the night before and go to sleep early

TWO TO ONE WEEK BEFORE

  • Apply for marriage license
  • Finalize arrangements with florist
  • Give final guests count to caterer (please consider the Photographers/Video personnel, band and/or DJ, wedding coordinator, etcetera)
  • Address and stamp wedding announcements
  • Break in wedding shoes at home
  • Pack for honeymoon
  • Confirm travel arrangements and pick up honeymoon tickets
  • Get blood test
  • Give gifts to wedding attendants

ONE MONTH BEFORE

  • Have gown and accessories delivered
  • Arrange name change on documents, if you wish
  • Order travelers checks for honeymoon
  • Send change of address notice to post office, if needed
  • Confirm rehearsal with church/site
  • Mail thank you notes for shower gifts
  • Call any guests who have not responded

SIX TO FOUR WEEKS BEFORE

  • Decide on your menu for the reception
  • Estimate the number of expected guests, including the Photographers/Video personnel, etcetera
  • Schedule a final fitting for the gown and tuxedo
  • Pick up your wedding rings
  • Purchase or borrow wedding accessories such as the ring pillow, toasting flutes, garter and candles
  • Plan the reception seating chart
  • Start writing place/sitting cards
  • Make sure all bridesmaid’s/groomsmen’s attire has been fitted

THREE MONTHS TO SIX WEEKS BEFORE

  • Prepare maps/directions for the ceremony and reception
  • Mail invitations
  • Schedule the gown and tuxedo fitting
  • Schedule sitting for the engagement/formal/casual portrait with the Photographer
  • Schedule rehearsals
  • Send Wedding announcements to the newspaper
  • Confirm wedding cake details with the baker
  • Discuss menu and décor with caterer
  • Buy a wedding guest book
  • Set the date and time for the rehearsal with the officiant
  • Finalize details with the florist, musicians, photographer and video personnel
  • Compile a request list for Band or DJ
  • Buy gifts for attendants

SIX TO THREE MONTHS BEFORE

  • Schedule appointments for physical exams with your doctor and dentist
  • Check requirements for blood test/marriage license in Pennsylvania or destination
  • Shop for wedding rings
  • Order wedding cake
  • Select and order invitations/stationery
  • Complete guest list
  • Begin shopping for your trousseau
  • Assist mothers/fathers/special relatives with selecting their dresses/tuxedos/suits
  • Select and order men’s attire
  • Reserve calligrapher/or purchase do it yourself programs/or printing company for invitations

NINE TO SIX MONTHS

  • Start compiling the guest list
  • Select/order bridesmaids’ dresses, tuxedos/suits for groomsmen
  • Shop for wedding dress, headpiece, veil
  • Start planning the honeymoon
  • Arrange wedding transportation
  • Register with the bridal department in your favorite store
  • Schedule rehearsal dinner

TWELVE TO NINE MONTHS BEFORE

  • Establish a preliminary budget
  • Determine the number of guests
  • Determine the type of wedding (size, formality, style, color scheme)
  • Determine who will officiate
  • Select a caterer, if not provided at the site
  • Select your attendants
  • Make lodging arrangements for out-of-state/town attendants
  • Select a baker and florist
  • Send engagement notice to the newspaper

EIGHTEEN TO TWELVE MONTHS BEFORE

  • Introduce both set of parents, if you haven’t already
  • Select Wedding date
  • Reserve church, temple or ceremony site
  • Vivendors.com
  • Shop for engagement ring
  • Reserve Professional Wedding/Portrait Photographer
  • Reserve Professional Video Personnel
  • Reserve reception facility
  • Contact a wedding consultant
  • Choose entertainment/hire musicians
