Company Profile
In 1996, I embarked on a career in photography and discovered my special niche in life. I started my photography career as an assistant, learning the basics and technical aspects, as well as the traditional way of photographing weddings. While quickly progressing from assistant to second to main photographer, I learned many aspects of the new approaches to photographing weddings--the photojournalistic, the documentary, the architectural, the romantic, the Fine Art, and the advertising approaches.
 
I have learned that my outgoing, eager personality is an asset in developing a rapport with the "client" which has helped me capture those "special moments". I find that I value and take pride in memorializing people's biggest life events. What I appreciate most is the ability to capture the spontaneous emotions and expressions of someone's wedding or special event day and presenting them with images that will allow you to preserve those feelings throughout your lives.
