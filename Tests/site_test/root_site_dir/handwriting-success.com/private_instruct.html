Private occupational therapy evaluation and intervention is available for children and teenagers.  Children, teens and adults can participate in private handwriting instruction. Private sessions are ideal for some students for a variety of reasons.  I often recommend private instruction for people who:
  • Have specific occupational therapy (i.e. sensory integration) needs
  • Benefit from reduced distraction
  • Live in the Downingtown area
  • simply prefer private session to the small group

The private sessions are usually 1-hour classes.

They are provided in the Downingtown office.

Sessions are usually scheduled weekly. 

Sessions are scheduled according to availability. 

Saturday sessions are available.
